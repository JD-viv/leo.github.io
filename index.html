What is Bike Service Tracker?

The Bike Service Tracker is a streamlined web application designed to help users manage and monitor the maintenance schedules of their bikes. Whether you're a casual rider or a bike enthusiast, this tool ensures your vehicle stays in peak condition by keeping track of:
  • Bike Details – Model name, year of manufacture, and personalized descriptions.
  • Service History – Last service date and upcoming service reminders.
  • User-Friendly Interface – Clean, responsive form inputs and dashboard views.
  • Dark Mode Aesthetic – Tailwind-powered styling with smooth fade-in animations.
  • Local Storage Integration – Saves entries directly in your browser for quick access.

This tracker is ideal for individuals managing multiple bikes or small workshops looking for a lightweight digital log. It’s built with modularity in mind, making it easy to expand with features like notifications, cloud sync, or service center integration.
